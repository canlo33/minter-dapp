The Birth of Hodl Up Society: A Tale of Greed, FOMO, and Laughter

The Hodl Up Society NFT collection is a hilarious and irreverent tribute to René Magritte's classic painting, "The Son of Man." We were inspired by the painting's exploration of the relationship between hidden and visible elements, and decided to bring it into the digital age.

But we didn't stop there. We also wanted to poke fun at the absurdity of our capitalist society and its never-ending quest for wealth and status. After all, if there's one thing that can make us laugh and cry at the same time, it's the endless pursuit of tendies.

Artist Behind Hold Up Society

Hodl Up Society is the artistic vision of Toronto-based artist Todd Monk, a rising star in the contemporary art world. His unique style and captivating figurative paintings are truly one of a kind and have touched the hearts of many art collectors.

Todd Monk is a highly respected painter with a wealth of experience and an impressive body of work. His paintings have been exhibited in some of the most prestigious galleries in cities around the world, including Toronto, Miami, London, New York, San Francisco, and Dallas.

His work has been featured in numerous publications, including Fuse Magazine and Spencer Magazine. He was named one of Art Business News' top 40 artists, a highly coveted honor in the art world. He was also the recipient of a Spotlight award at Spectrum/Red Dot Miami Art Fair in 2019, a testament to his growing reputation in the industry.
